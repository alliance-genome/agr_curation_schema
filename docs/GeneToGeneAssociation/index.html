Class: GeneToGeneAssociation

abstract parent class for different kinds of gene-gene or gene product to gene product relationships. Includes homology and interaction.

URI: alliance:GeneToGeneAssociation

Parents

  • is_a: Association - A typed association between two entities, supported by evidence.

Children

  • GeneInteraction - An interaction between two genes or gene products; this may be a physical molecular interaction between gene products (e.g. protein-protein interactions), or may be a genetic interaction between genes (e.g. phenotypic suppression)
